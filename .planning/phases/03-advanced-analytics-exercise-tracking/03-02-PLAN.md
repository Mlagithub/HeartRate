3. Implementation details:
   - Query: `SELECT COUNT(*), AVG(bpm), AVG(bpm * bpm) FROM heart_rate_records WHERE ...`
   - Variance = AVG(bpm*bpm) - AVG(bpm)^2
   - RMSSD approximation: sqrt(variance) * 10 (scaling factor to ms range, Claude's discretion)
   - Confidence levels:
     - "high": >100 data points
     - "medium": 50-100 data points
     - "low": <50 data points
   - is_estimated: always true per D-11
